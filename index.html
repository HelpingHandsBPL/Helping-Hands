About Us

Dedicated to providing essential food aid to families in Gaza

Helping Hands is bhopal based humanitarian organization committed to alleviating hunger and providing essential food assistance to families in Gaza. Founded on principles of compassion and solidarity, we work tirelessly to ensure that no family goes without basic nutrition during these challenging times.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to deliver life-sustaining food aid directly to those who need it most. Through our network of local partners and volunteers, we identify families in critical need and provide them with nutritious food packages that can sustain them for weeks.

Every donation, every package, and every meal represents hope. We believe that in our shared humanity, no one should face hunger alone, and that together, we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of Gaza's most vulnerable families.

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by compassion, driven by action

Our Mission

To provide immediate and sustained food assistance to families in Gaza, ensuring that basic nutritional needs are met during times of crisis. We work with transparency, efficiency, and unwavering commitment to human dignity.

Our Vision

A world where no family in Gaza faces hunger. We envision communities where children can grow healthy and strong, where families have the security of knowing their next meal is assured, and where hope prevails over hardship.

Our Values

Compassion drives our every action. Transparency guides our operations. Dignity shapes our approach. Efficiency maximizes our impact. Together, these values ensure that every donation creates meaningful change in the lives of those we serve.

Stories of Hope

Real families, real impact, real hope restored

The Abu Khalil Family

Restoring Dignity Through Nutrition

A family of seven including elderly grandparents and three young children received monthly food packages for six months. The nutritious meals helped the children regain their strength and return to their studies, while providing the elderly family members with the specialized nutrition they needed.

Um Mahmoud's Story

A Mother's Relief

Um Mahmoud, a widow caring for four children, received consistent food aid that allowed her to focus on her children's education rather than worry about their next meal. Her eldest daughter is now able to attend school regularly and dreams of becoming a teacher.

Community Kitchen Project

Feeding an Entire Neighborhood

Our community kitchen initiative in the Zeitoun neighborhood provides fresh, hot meals to over 200 families daily. This program not only addresses immediate hunger but also creates a sense of community and shared support among neighbors.
